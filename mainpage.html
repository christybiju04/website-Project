A DOG'S TALE



Thinking about adopting a pet? Great news! You are embarking on a wonderful experience, not just for the dog or cat, but for you and your family as well. Few things are as rewarding as rescuing a precious life with four legs! As a family-owned company with our “ear to the ground”, PetTest knows that there are sometimes dogs and cats in need of a little extra care – diabetic pets, or as we prefer, “Sugar Babies” – that are up for adoption. The sad reality is that many people are afraid of the extra expertise and expenses diabetic dogs and cats bring with them. This is precisely where PetTest is uniquely positioned to help!

We are proud to announce that PetTest will provide the initial testing equipment and supplies to enrolled individuals who adopt a diabetic pet, making for a stress-free experience. We will also give you full access to our community partners to help educate you on the care of your new Sugar Baby.
